1. Introduction

Welcome to the kJams web interface! This applications allows you to choose and organize the songs you wish to sing tonight. Create a new singer account or login to an account your've already created and you're ready to go! Once you've logged in, start by entering a search and dragging songs you want into your Tonight playlist.

2. Browsing Playlists

To view songs in a playlist, simply click the playlist; the songs will then laod in the main window. You can then drag to rearrange them (except in History, and Library) as well as add them to your Tonight or other playlists. You can also change the pitch of songs in your Tonight playlist.

3. Accessing and Searching the Library

Before you can view songs in the Library, you need to enter a search to avoid getting back huge song lists. Up at the top, you should see a field labled "Search Library." Enter part of song title, artist, or album and press Go. Your search results will appear in the main window and you can then drag them into your playlists.

4. Adding Songs to Your Playlists

You can add songs to any of your playlists except History and Library. To do so, simply click and drag the song over to the playlist you want and release the mouse button. For the Tonight and Favorites playlists, you can click the "Tonight" and "Favorites" buttons in the main window.

5. Arranging Songs Within Your Playlists

To rearrange the order of songs in any of your playlists (except History and Library), simply click and drag a song to a new spot in the list and release the mouse button.

6. Removing Songs From Your Playlists

To remove a song from any of your playlists (except History and Library), select the song you wish to remove, and press Del on the keyboard (usually fn + Delete on laptop keyboards).
